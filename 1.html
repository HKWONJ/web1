매운갈비찜

future is female
in the good old days when wishes often came true, there lived a king whose daughters were so beautiful that even the sun, who had seen so much beauty, was surprised every time he kissed her face. close to the king home was a dark forest. in the forest, under an old tree was a watcher-hole. the princess often went there on hot days, to sit on the bank by the cool water. she loved playing with a special golden ball which she would

draw high in the air and then catch.
